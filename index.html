About

Wait Actually Uploader is the first-party publishing tool behind the Wait, Actually channel — a faceless short-form series that busts one common myth a day with a surprising, science-backed fact in under 60 seconds. The uploader posts our own original videos to our own accounts automatically via the official platform APIs.

The app

Wait Actually Uploader uploads our own original short videos directly to our own social accounts via the official platform APIs. It only ever posts our own content to our own accounts and does not access other users' data.
