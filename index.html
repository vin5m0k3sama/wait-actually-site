About

Wait, Actually is a faceless short-form video channel. Every day we bust one common myth with a surprising, science-backed fact — all in under 60 seconds. The kind of thing that makes you stop and go "wait... actually?!"

Where to watch

New Shorts every day on YouTube and TikTok. Comment a topic and we'll cover it next.

The app

"Wait, Actually" runs an automated publishing tool that uploads our own original short videos directly to our own social accounts via the official platform APIs. The tool only ever posts our own content to our own accounts and does not access other users' data.
