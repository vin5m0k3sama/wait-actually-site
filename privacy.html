Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Wait, Actually" publishing application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information. The App is operated by the owner of the "Wait, Actually" channel and is used solely to publish the owner's own original videos to the owner's own social media accounts (such as YouTube and TikTok).

1. Information we access

When you (the account owner) connect a social media account, the App uses the platform's official OAuth authorization to obtain an access token and a refresh token. These tokens are used only to upload videos to your own account on your behalf.

  • We access the minimum account information required to publish a video (such as your account's allowed privacy settings returned by the platform).
  • We do not collect, store, sell, or share your personal data, contacts, messages, or any other users' information.
  • We do not use the App to read, analyze, or access content belonging to other people.

2. How tokens are stored

Authorization tokens are stored locally on the owner's own computer (in a local file) and are used only to authenticate upload requests to the platform. They are not transmitted to any third party other than the platform that issued them (e.g. TikTok or YouTube).

3. How we use information

The only purpose is to upload the owner's own short videos and their captions/titles to the owner's own connected accounts. We do not use the information for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

4. Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Information is exchanged only directly between the App and the official APIs of the platforms you connect, in order to perform the upload you requested.

5. Data retention and deletion

Tokens remain stored locally until you revoke access or delete them. You can revoke the App's access at any time from your platform's settings (for TikTok: Settings → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions), and you can delete the local token file at any time.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
